Dried Okra Soup

Dried okra is a nothern Nigerian delicacy, it's origins have been disputed with some
argueing it originates from the north eastern part of the country. The main ingredient is
(you guessed right!) sun dried okra which is blended to a powder, the powder can be coarse or fine depending on
your preference.

How to cook

the recipe begins with making a broth base, which can be beef, chicken or mutton broth,
fry your pepper and onion, add mutton, beef or chicken or any protein of your choice,
season with spices to taste boil protein until soft and broth is thicken, add cold water (this prevents okra powder from clumping)
and add dried okra powder to your desired thickness, allow to simmer and serve with your preferred starch.

Chicken stew

The star of this dish is the chicken however quality ingredients to make stew enhances
its taste. chicken stew is a global dish with recipes varying in different parts of the world.
The Nigerian chicken chicken-stew is spicy.

How to cook

fresh chicken cut into pieces is boiled with spices and seasoning of your choice, drain
chicken and fry in vegetable oil and keep aside. Blend fresh tomatoes, onion, cayene pepper(shombo) and red bell peppers(tatashe),to a paste
and fry in leftover oil used to fry chicken until paste is thick and devoid of water. Add broth, fried chicken, season to taste and bring to a
simmer. Serve with rice or rice and beans.

Chocolate cake

Chocolate cake is also a global dish with varying recipes according to location, in its most
basic recipe which you will find here it is fool proof, delicious, and easy to make. it contains simple ingredients you can find anywhere.

How to cook

Start by pre-heating your oven to 180 degrees and lining baking pan with baking paper. Mix dry
ingredients which include equal amounts of flour and sugar, add a three-quarter cup of cocoa powder
a teaspoon each of baking powder and baking soda, mix well and keep aside. Next you mix your wet
ingredients which includes half a cup of milk, quarter cup of oil and one large egg.
mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients until just combined (do not over mix!), add a cup of
boiling hot instant coffee and mix until just combined too. Pour mixture into baking pan and bake for 30 minutes.
